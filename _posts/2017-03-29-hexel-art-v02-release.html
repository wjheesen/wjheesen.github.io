--- layout: post title: Hexel Art v0.2 Release date: 2017-03-29 ---

I'm pleased to announce the release of Hexel Art v0.2, an improvement over the prototype I released in an earlier post. The new release features a grid size of upto 512x512 hexels, undo/redo options, semi-transparent draw colors, and a more elegant UI.
